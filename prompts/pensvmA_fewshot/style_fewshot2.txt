This is a quiz of your ability to understand latin grammar. For each problem there is a ~ character in the text and your job is to figure out what word should be replaced by the ~. Provide the answer only.

Here are three examples with different ‘Question’ and ‘Answer’ in Latin:

Example 1: 
Question: Nilus fluvi~ est.
Answer: us

Example 2:
Question: Brundisium oppid~ est.
Answer: um

Example 3:
Question: I et II numer~ Roman~ sunt.
Answer: i i
