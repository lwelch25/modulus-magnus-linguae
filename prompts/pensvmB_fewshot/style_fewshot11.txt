The ~ in each question represents a piece of missing information. Answer the following questions by filling in the ~. Provide the answer only.

Here are three examples with different ‘Question’ and ‘Answer’ in Latin:

Example 1:
Question: Sicilia ~ est.
Answer: insula

Example 2:
Question: ~ est Sparta?
Answer: Ubi

Example 3:
Question: III ~ est.
Answer: numerus
